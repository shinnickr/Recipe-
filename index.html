Sweet Crêpe Recipe

Recipe by Robin Shinnick
Website by Rory Shinnick

Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 2-4 min per
Serving size: Approx. 2-4 people



Ingredients
    ¼ cup sugar (granulated)
    ½ cup water
    ½ cup milk
    ¼ tsp salt
    2 eggs
    2 tbsp butter (melted)
    1 cup flour
Toppings (optional)
    Powdered sugar
    Granulated sugar
    Nutella
    Bananas
    Strawberries
    Syrup
    Peanut Butter
Instructions
    1) In a bowl, combine and mix together the sugar, water, milk, salt, eggs. In a sauté pan, melt the butter then add the melted butter to the mixture (stir when adding). Sift the flour into the batter, stirring in periodically.

    2) Heat a lightly oiled sauté pan over medium heat, when warm, add a ¼ cup of batter and lift the pan and rotate in circular motion so the batter spreads evenly. Cook until the bottom is light brown, flip, and serve hot.

    3) Enjoy!
