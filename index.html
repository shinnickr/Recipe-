Crepe Recipe

Recipe by Robin Shinnick
Ingredients
    ¼ cup sugar (granulated) ½ cup water ½ cup milk ¼ tsp salt 2 eggs 2 tbsp butter (melted) 1 cup flour
    Toppings (optional) Powdered sugar Granulated sugar Nutella Bananas Strawberries Syrup Peanut Butter

    Instructions In a bowl, combine and mix together the sugar, water, milk, salt, eggs. In a sauté pan, melt the butter then add the melted butter to the mixture (stir when adding). Sift the flour into the batter, stirring in periodically.

    Heat a lightly oiled sauté pan over medium heat, when warm, add a ¼ cup of batter and lift the pan and rotate in circular motion so the batter spreads evenly. Cook until the bottom is light brown, flip, and serve hot
